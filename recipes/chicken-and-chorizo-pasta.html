Chicken and Chorizo Pasta

Home

Description

A classic dish for those who enjoy some heat. This dish elevates the humble chicken breast with the sharp, spicy taste of Spanish chorizo, balanced within a rich pomodoro sauce and complemented by a generous portion of grana padano cheese.

Ingredients

  • Roasted chicken breast
  • Chorizo sausage
  • Cherry tomatoes (Picollato)
  • Fresh basil
  • Tinned tomatoes
  • Red bell Pepper
  • Penne pasta
  • Freshly ground black pepper
  • Table salt
