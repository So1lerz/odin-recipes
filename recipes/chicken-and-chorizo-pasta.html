Chicken and Chorizo Pasta

Home

Description

A classic dish for those who enjoy some heat. This dish elevates the humble chicken breast with the sharp, spicy taste of Spanish chorizo, balanced within a rich pomodoro sauce and complemented by a generous portion of grana padano cheese.

Ingredients

  • Roasted chicken breast
  • Chorizo sausage
  • Cherry tomatoes (Picollato)
  • Fresh basil
  • Tinned tomatoes
  • Red bell Pepper
  • Penne pasta
  • Freshly ground black pepper
  • Table salt

Steps

  1. Chop peppers into a large dice, and pat down with a paper towel to remove excess moisture and reserve
  2. Chop cherry tomatoes into halves and reserve
  3. Dice the chorizo and cut the roasted chicken into strips
  4. Prepare a colander in a nearby sink, and heat two pans, a large saucepan and a wok on high heat. In the saucepan, fill with water and when the wok is warm, add a glug of olive oil
  5. Add salt to the saucepan until the water is salty. If you have an extraction fan, turn this on as the water comes to a rolling boil
  6. When the olive oil begins to simmer, add the chorizo, season with black pepper, and sear until crispy
  7. Add peppers to the pan and continue to sear to blister the peppers and add some colour to the skins
  8. Add the penne pasta to the pasta water and drop the pan temperature to a slow boil, and cook for 8 minutes until al dente
  9. Add cherry tomatoes to the pan when sufficient colour is achieved on the chorizo and pepper
  10. Cook through the cherry tomatoes until they begin to break away from their skins, which is usually several minutes and press down with an implement during cooking to release their juices when sufficiently soft. The pectin in the cherry tomatoes with help bind the sauce.
  11. Take a ladle and add half a ladle of starch water from the pasta pot to the wok
  12. Keep the wok on high heat and add tinned tomatoes to the pan, and break the tomatoes into small pieces with an implement while cooking
  13. Cook through the sauce in the pan until it has reduced by half, if the pasta has cooked before this, drain in the colander and reserve until this has been achieved
  14. Add chicken and pasta to the pan and combine for several minutes until heated through
  15. Add a knob of butter to finish and melt in some grana padano cheese to enhance the flavour
  16. Serve onto a warm plate if possible
